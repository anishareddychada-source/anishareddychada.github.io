About Data Analyst Role

A Data Analyst is a professional who transforms raw data into meaningful insights that help organizations make better business decisions. Data Analysts use tools like Excel, SQL, Python, and Power BI to clean, analyze, and visualize data.

The core responsibilities include collecting and cleaning data, identifying patterns and trends, creating reports or dashboards, and communicating findings to stakeholders in a clear and actionable way. They play a key role in bridging technical data and business strategy.

Common projects include analyzing customer feedback, sales performance, financial metrics, and operational efficiency. Data Analysts often work closely with Data Engineers and Business Analysts to build data-driven solutions that improve decision-making.
